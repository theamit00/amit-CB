CAMPUS SUPERHERO

A program aimed at identifying energetic, passionate, brilliant, like minded College Superheroes who want to network, empower, learn and grow

Get Started

Objectives of program?

Campus Superheros are the face of Coding Blocks in their campus. Our Community conducts regular sessions in colleges for the student community to hone their Tech skills. In addition, members of the community learn leadership and soft skills which come in handy in future. Campus Superheroes also receive specific perks, referrals and endorsements from Leadership.

Be the face of Coding Blocks

Campus Superheroes represent Coding Blocks in their campus and help the Student community by organising workshops and Hackathons which hones Tech skills on students in their campus.

Conduct Workshops, Hackathons & more

Amazon coming to your campus to hire? Alumni of Coding Blocks can come and do a webinar on how to level up and crack the company. Many Workshops and webinars of similar type can be conducted for different years for skillification.

Online Contests using Hackerblocks

If the college has a need for platform where they could conduct Tests and do evaluation, then Coding Blocks can help with their In-house Coding platform. This is a very regular and on-going practice and CB loves to assist College professors and students

Create an active Community

Network...Learn...Empower...Grow is the foundation of the Community program at Coding Blocks. Knowledge and opportunity should be accessible to all and we do our best to promote and implement it.
